Welcome to Tilak Education Society
Tilak Education Society was established in 1984 with the aim of promoting education in the city of Navi Mumbai. It was registered under the Society’s Act and also registered as an Educational Trust under Public Trust Act 1950. Since inception the society is engaged in educational activities from Nursery to Post-Graduation level in Navi Mumbai. Today the society runs 10 institutions in 3 different educational complexes in Navi Mumbai catering to the educational needs of over 10,000 students in Navi Mumbai. All our complexes are located at important nodes in Navi Mumbai city i.e Vashi, Nerul, Ghansoli.
