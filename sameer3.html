Facilities


Tilak Education Society provides maximum facilities for the all round development of the personality of the students.

College Library


Tilak Education Society has made a very good library for the students with a large number of books, comprising of textbooks, reference books, magazines, periodicals, newspapers etc. A full- time librarian with an assistant renders helping hand to the students in their search for the selection of books which are indeed the store-house of knowledge to improve their standard and score well in the exam. Our libraries functions from 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. during all the working days.

Science Laboratories



Every institute our society is well equipped with various science laboratories.Five well equipped Science Labs – Physics, Chemistry, Biology,Biotechnology and Tissue Culture which fulfill the need of the students. Efficient teachers and a separate lab assistant for each lab guide and instruct the students to do their best in the examination.

Information Technology Laboratories


Computer Lab 1 PC for every student has been the aim of our society. Computer labs in our college are well equipped with all the necessary infrastructure and software to enable our students to excel in IT space. All concepts cant be explained on computers alone, our Electronics lab give the students a peak into the world of various components and hardware used in Information Technology sector.

Gymkhana


All our institutes have been gifted with a very large playground where the students are given coaching for Cricket, Hockey, Football, Volleyball etc. There is a separate basketball ground for the students’ maximum use. Apart from outdoor games, indoor games like Chess, Carom etc. are also made available for the students. The Gymkhana encourages the students to take part in developing their sportive spirit & athletic skills. It also conducts various tournaments of indoor games within the college and prepares the students to participate in various Inter- collegiate tournaments at Zonal/State/National level.

Scholarships & Prizes



Prizes are awarded to the toppers in each division on the basis of the average marks of the year. Similarly Tilak Education Society provides free student-ships and half-fee concessions to economically backward and meritorious students. T.E.S. also gives fee concessions and scholarship to students who have proved their talent in the field of sports and games at district and state level.

Magzine

To encourage the writing skills of the students, the every institure brings out an annual issue of the college magazine at the end of the second term of every academic year. Besides chosen articles, poem etc. of students, it also contains a glimpse of various co-curricular activities and achievements of the students during the academic year.

Audio Visual


Quality Education through latest teaching aids has been the aim of our society. All our institutes have an audio-visual room with audio- visual teaching aids to impart teaching very effectively.
