Milestones


1984 : Tilak Education Society was established
1985 : Vashi English School was started
1989 : Junior College Section Commerce was started in Vashi
1992 : Head Office was shifted to new premise Kurup’s Educational Complex, Vashi
1992 : Vocation Education was started in Vashi branch
2001 : Junior College Science was started in Vashi branch
2002 : Tilak College Vashi was established Affiliated to Mumbai University having Commerce stream(B.Com),BMS and Computer Science(C.S)
2003 : Biotech department was started in Vashi campus
2003 : BMM and BSc.IT departments were started in Vashi campus
2003 : Tilak Public School was established in Nerul affiliated to CBSE Board, Delhi
2004 : Tilak International School was started in Ghansoli
2004 : Kurup’s Vidyanagar Campus was inaugurated in Nerul
2005 : Smt. Sarada Kurup Educational Complex was inaugurated in Ghansoli
2006 : Tilak Junior College was established in Nerul with Science, Arts & Commerce streams affiliated to Maharashtra Board
2007 : Tilak Junior College was established in Ghansoli with Science & Commerce streams affiliated to Maharashtra Board
2008 : S.K College of Science & Commerce was established in Nerul affiliated to Mumbai University
2009 : J.K College,Ghansoli was established with Science & Commerce faculty affiliated to Mumbai University
2009 : S.K College of Education and Research was established in Ghansoli recognized by NCTE and affiliated to Mumbai University
2012 : Bifocal streams(CS/Electronics) were introduced in Vashi and Nerul
2013 : Tilak Public School started 11th classes affiliated to CBSE board, Delhi
