From The Chairmen's Desk
(Founder Chairman)
Tilak Education Society was founded in 1984 to provide holistic education with Indian Orientation. Continuous pursuits of excellence has been the foundation stone of our Institution work culture. It is our responsive strategy, carefully drawn-out plans, total commitment and dedication that has helped us to attain unhampered growth meritorious success and recognition as some of the best educational institutes in Navi Mumbai. We cannot, ofcourse, achieve all we aspire to without the blessing of God Almighty, along with the active support of our parents. we hope that Almighty will always guide and aspire us to achieve greater height of excellence.

From The Vice Chairman's Desk

SHRI. AJIT J. KURUP - B.E., M.M.S. (H.R),Mphil

 (Vice-Chairman & Director) “A leader is one who knows the way, goes the way, and shows the way.” —John Maxwell It’s this wonderful thought which has been the guiding force behind Tilak Education Society’s vision of moulding students into responsible leaders of the future. Today, through our various institutes we cater to the academic needs of thousands of students from kindergarten to post graduate level. But only academic knowledge cannot produce leaders; holistic development of students is the key. Our institutions equipped with quality infrastructure and experienced faculty try to focus upon areas of specialization, technology, professional competence and ability to adapt and excel in dynamic environment. The emphasis here is to go beyond the limits of the curriculum to provide practical knowledge and through various academic, cultural and sports events instil a sense of confidence in the individual to perform in every field at the highest level. Creating the right set of moral values, tolerance and social commitments is an integral part of our education. We believe that armed with the power of knowledge, self belief and right principles our students would be able to become leaders of tomorrow. With the same ideals we have been adding new institutes to our fold. All this would not have been possible without the blessing of the Almighty, efforts of the principals, staff, students and support of the parents and well wishers.

From The Honsecretary's Desk

SHRI. ARUN JANARDHAN - B.E, M.M.S(H.R)
(Hon. Secretary) Tilak Education Society is registered on 21st September 1984 under the Societies Act of 1860 and Bombay Public Trust Act 1950. The prime objective of the society is to provide all facilities to the children of the area to enhance their studies in the most congenial atmosphere. The society strongly believes that investment in quality care and education for young minds represent a significant investment in the future well being of the society. The society has all along kept pace with the steadily increasing need of education at all levels. i.e. from nursery to college level. Owing to the vision and tireless efforts of its members and the unstinted support of its well wishers, the society is able to develop many institutions from nursery to college level for the requirement of different sections and linguistic people. Our institution aims to provide the best for all the young people of varied abilities, encouraging the to achieve standards of excellence in all spheres of activities, from academic to sport and aesthetics to practical.
