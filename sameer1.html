About us

Tilak Education Society was established in 1984 with the aim of promoting education in the city of Navi Mumbai. It was registered under the Society’s Act and also registered as an Educational Trust under Public Trust Act 1950. Since inception the society is engaged in educational activities from Nursery to Post-Graduation level in Navi Mumbai. Today the society runs 10 institutions in 3 different educational complexes in Navi Mumbai catering to the educational needs of over 10,000 students in Navi Mumbai.

Vision

“To develop world class institutions for holistic development of individuals involving Academic excellence , professional competence , human values and societal skills to empower them to become leaders capable of facing future challenges and guiding the society”.

Mission

To import Quality Education by providing the best infrastructure combined with innovative pedagogy for an all-round development of individuals To run academic programmes from nursery to post-graduate level in the city so as to provide an individual with an avenue for being professionally and globally competent. To harness leadership potential in students and provide a platform for the same. To be a centre for learning and technological awareness in the city of Navi Mumbai

Motto

"Nurturing Tommorrow's Mind"
